A designated project manager leads your project

When you work with Chapter, you have an experienced project manager (PM) overseeing all phases of your renovation project. Our PMs are educated in construction-related fields, like civil engineering and architecture, which makes them truly qualified to lead your project. Serving as a central point of contact, our PMs efficiently manage all vendors, subcontractors, and suppliers, ensuring a seamless and well-coordinated execution.

Our design team
captures your style

Our in-house interior designers work with you to achieve a high-end design that’s tailored to your personal preferences, at no additional costs. They collaborate seamlessly with your project manager to assure an efficient process. With a shared commitment to excellence, our designers, who are all backed by impressive credentials and extensive experience, ensure a quality, personalized design.
