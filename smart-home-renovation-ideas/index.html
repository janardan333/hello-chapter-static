Top 5 smart home renovation ideas: upgrading your home with technology

Gabriela Flores
Project Manager

Renovating your home is not just about aesthetics; it's about making your life easier. As technology continues to advance, there are countless ways to incorporate smart features into your home that will not only improve convenience but also enhance your daily experience. Here are some of the latest tech-driven innovations that can transform your space and improve your life.

1. Smart toilets: say goodbye to scrubbing

Imagine a toilet that cleans itself after every use. No more scrubbing or worrying about stains. Smart toilets come equipped with built-in cleaning mechanisms that use advanced systems like water jets, UV light, and even electrolyzed water to sanitize the bowl automatically. With features like heated seats, bidet functions, and even air purifiers, smart toilets combine convenience and cleanliness, saving you time and effort while elevating your bathroom experience.

Price range: Basic to mid-tier can range between $1,000-$4,000, while high-end options can exceed $4,000 and reach up to $10,000.

2. Smart light switches: effortless lighting control

Gone are the days of fumbling for light switches in the dark. With smart light switches, you can program the lights in your home to turn on automatically as soon as you enter a room, thanks to motion sensors. You can also create custom "scenes" for different times of day or activities. Want bright lighting when you're getting ready for work? Set your lights to a higher brightness. Ready to wind down for bed? Dim the lights to a soft glow to create a relaxing ambiance. These small adjustments not only help conserve energy but also improve the mood and functionality of your home.

Price range: Generally, range from $30 to $100 per switch.
Note: Professional installation may add extra costs.

3. Smart appliances: efficiency at your fingertips

In today's fast-paced world, time is precious. Smart appliances such as stoves, ranges, washers, and dryers are designed to make life more efficient. With a smart oven, you can preheat your oven remotely while you're still at the grocery store. Smart washers and dryers can send you alerts when your laundry is done, and even allow you to schedule wash cycles to fit your busy lifestyle. These appliances are built to integrate with your smartphone, giving you full control over your household tasks, no matter where you are.

Price range: Small appliances can start around $500 while major appliances (e.g., smart ovens, refrigerators, washers/dryers): typically range from $1,000 to $4,000 per unit.

4. Smart furniture considerations for renovations

When undergoing a renovation, it's also important to consider how your furniture will work in harmony with your tech. For instance, if you plan to integrate a robot vacuum, consider open spaces or minimalistic furniture that allows the vacuum to move freely. Additionally, opting for smart furniture like desks with built-in charging ports, adjustable height features, or even couches with integrated speakers can further enhance your home’s functionality. A well-thought-out layout that accommodates both your furniture and tech devices can make your home feel more streamlined and efficient.

Price range: Prices can vary widely; for example, smart desks or tech-integrated couches usually range from $300 to over $2,000.

Note: These ranges are approximate and can vary based on brand, features, and installation services.

The power of integration: connecting your smart devices

When all of your devices are connected, they can work together to streamline your life even further. For instance, imagine walking into your home, and as you enter the front door, your smart thermostat adjusts the temperature, the lights turn on to your preferred brightness, and your smart speakers play your favorite music. Integration of all your smart devices can create a seamless, effortless living environment that adapts to your needs.

Small Details, Big Impact

It’s the little things that make a big difference. While technology may seem like a luxury, when it’s thoughtfully integrated into your home, it becomes an everyday convenience that improves your life. Whether it’s a self-cleaning toilet that eliminates the hassle of maintenance, smart lighting that adjusts to your schedule, or energy-efficient appliances that save you time and money, these details add up to create a home that’s not only smarter but more comfortable and efficient. By hiring a technology-driven renovation company like Chapter, you can ensure that your home is at the forefront of modern living. As a Renotech company, we’re always looking for ways to innovate and incorporate technology into the renovation experience.
