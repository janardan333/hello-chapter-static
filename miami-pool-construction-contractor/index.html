Pool construction in Miami: What’s involved

Building a pool in Miami requires careful planning, precise compliance with building codes, and selecting materials suited to South Florida’s climate. Chapter oversees every detail, from design and permitting through to construction, ensuring your pool meets your vision, budget, and Miami’s unique requirements.

Consultation & project planning

Our team meets with you to understand your vision, preferences, and budget. We conduct site assessments to tailor designs specifically suited to your property and local conditions.

Permits & compliance

Chapter navigates Miami’s complex permitting process, handling all documentation, submissions, and approvals efficiently, streamlining your pool installation timeline.

Pool design & engineering

Our designers and engineers create detailed architectural and engineering plans for your pool, incorporating desired features, functionality, and compliance with Miami’s building codes.

Site excavation & preparation

We carefully clear and excavate your site, preparing the ground properly to ensure a stable foundation for your pool and efficient installation.

Structural shell installation

Chapter constructs the pool’s structural shell, incorporating steel reinforcements, plumbing, and electrical lines precisely to your design specifications.

Pool surface finishes & tiling

Our experts apply high-quality finishes and tile work, selecting materials that withstand Miami’s climate while ensuring your pool looks stunning for years.

Decking & outdoor features

We install beautiful, weather-resistant decking, such as travertine, concrete pavers, or natural stone, and enhance your pool area with outdoor living features tailored to your lifestyle.

Equipment & systems setup

Chapter installs and configures essential pool equipment, including pumps, filtration systems, heaters, lighting, and smart automation features for seamless operation and easy maintenance.

Quality assurance & final inspections

Upon completion, Chapter conducts thorough quality assurance checks, manages final city inspections, and ensures all permits are properly closed, guaranteeing your pool meets all local codes and your expectations.
