Best home decor stores in Miami

Irit Andiroglu
Interior Designer

Miami is more than just a tropical paradise, it’s a hub for style and design. Whether you’re furnishing a luxury condo with panoramic ocean views or a cozy apartment in the heart of the city, Miami’s home decor stores offer something for everyone. From high-end showrooms to eclectic boutiques, this city is brimming with options to elevate your interiors. In this guide, we’re sharing our top five home decor stores in Miami, each with its unique flair and offerings:

1. Artefacto

Why it's a must-visit
Artefacto is synonymous with luxury and sophistication, offering minimalist and contemporary pieces perfect for high-end homes or condos. The brand’s commitment to sustainability adds a modern touch.

Best for
High-end furniture and elegant accents, with a focus on neutral palettes and clean lines.

Price range: $$$$
Expect to invest for quality; sofas range from $5,000+, while smaller decor starts at $300.

Location highlight
Their Coral Gables showroom feels like a curated art gallery, showcasing dreamy layouts and an impressive range of finishes.

Pro tip
Artefacto often holds exclusive design events—great for networking with Miami’s interior design elite.

2. The Bazaar Project

Why it's a must-visit
This concept store in Coconut Grove combines boho-chic home accents with curated lifestyle items like jewelry and art. It’s perfect for adding a touch of global flair to your space.

Best for
Unique, handcrafted items like Moroccan rugs, Turkish ceramics, and quirky decorative pieces.

Price range: $$
Affordable luxury, with decor items ranging from $50 to $500.

What to know
The store’s eclectic vibe makes it ideal for finding conversation-starting decor.

Pro tip
Look out for seasonal pop-up events featuring international artisans.

3. Design Within Reach (DWR)

Why it's a must-visit
Known for making modern design accessible, Design Within Reach offers a stunning array of iconic furniture, lighting, and decor from top designers like Eames, Knoll, and Herman Miller. Their modern aesthetic suits both minimalists and fans of timeless elegance.

Best for
Mid-century modern furniture, ergonomic office chairs, and statement lighting.

Price range: $$$
Premium quality at competitive prices; classic chairs start at $1,000, and smaller decor like vases or trays begins around $100.

What Stands Out
DWR's Brickell showroom is a paradise for design enthusiasts, offering a tactile experience to see and feel pieces in person.

Pro tip
Check their sale section or outlet for significant discounts on high-end designs.

4. CB2 – Miami Beach

Why it's a must-visit
This chic offshoot of Crate & Barrel brings trendy, affordable modern design to the heart of Lincoln Road.

Best for
Stylish furniture and decor for young professionals or first-time decorators looking for on-trend pieces.

Price range: $$
Mid-range pricing; coffee tables start at $300, while smaller decor like vases and trays begin at $25.

What Stands Out
CB2 often collaborates with renowned designers to offer exclusive collections.

Pro tip
Shop during seasonal sales for even better deals on their coveted decor.

5. Calimia Home

Why it's a must-visit
A boutique design studio turned home decor haven, Calimia Home offers a curated collection of furnishings and accessories that exude modern elegance with a coastal touch, perfect for Miami's aesthetic.

Best for
Customizable pieces, high-quality textiles, and carefully selected decor that balances sophistication and comfort.

Price range: $$-$$$
Accent pieces like pillows start at $75, while larger items, such as coffee tables or custom furniture, can range from $500+.

What Stands Out
Calimia Home's curated approach feels personal, with a focus on timeless design and materials that elevate any space. Their neutral tones and natural textures pair effortlessly with Miami’s tropical lifestyle.
